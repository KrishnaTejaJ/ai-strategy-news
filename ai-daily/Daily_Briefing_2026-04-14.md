## 1. The US military needs a commercial backup plan for an AI chip crisis
**Source:** Center for Security and Emerging Technology  
**Link:** [The US military needs a commercial backup plan for an AI chip crisis](https://cset.georgetown.edu/article/the-us-military-needs-a-commercial-backup-plan-for-an-ai-chip-crisis/)  

**So What:** US military readiness may be compromised by AI chip supply chain disruptions, highlighting the need for a commercial backup plan

The US military's ability to rapidly retrain AI models in crises is critical for battlefield adaptability, and supply chain disruptions could significantly impact this capability. A commercial backup plan could mitigate these risks, but it would require significant investment and coordination with industry partners. The development of such a plan would also need to consider the potential risks of relying on commercial suppliers for critical military capabilities

**Watch For:** US government initiatives to develop a commercial backup plan for AI chip supply chains and potential partnerships with industry leaders
