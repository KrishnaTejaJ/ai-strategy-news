## 3. The US military needs a commercial backup plan for an AI chip crisis
**Source:** Center for Security and Emerging Technology  
**Link:** [The US military needs a commercial backup plan for an AI chip crisis](https://cset.georgetown.edu/article/the-us-military-needs-a-commercial-backup-plan-for-an-ai-chip-crisis/)  

**So What:** This matters because it highlights the critical challenge of AI chip supply chain resilience for US military readiness

The ability to rapidly retrain AI models in crises is crucial for US military adaptability, and the current reliance on specialized AI chips poses a significant risk. Developing a commercial backup plan could mitigate this risk, but it would require significant investment and coordination between the military, industry, and government agencies. This challenge is closely tied to the broader issue of semiconductor supply chain security.

**Watch For:** US military initiatives to develop alternative AI chip solutions and strengthen supply chain resilience
