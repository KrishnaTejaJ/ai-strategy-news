## 1. China Seeks A.I. Independence, Weakening Trump’s Leverage
**Source:** Center for Security and Emerging Technology  
**Link:** [China Seeks A.I. Independence, Weakening Trump’s Leverage](https://cset.georgetown.edu/article/china-seeks-a-i-independence-weakening-trumps-leverage/)  

**So What:** China's efforts to build a domestic AI ecosystem undermine US leverage in the tech competition.

China's push for AI independence is a strategic move to reduce reliance on US technology, which could limit the effectiveness of US export controls. This development is significant as it highlights China's determination to become self-sufficient in critical technologies. The success of Chinese companies like DeepSeek and Huawei in developing alternatives to American chips could have far-reaching implications for the global tech landscape.

**Watch For:** Monitor China's progress in developing its domestic AI ecosystem and the impact on US-China tech competition.
