## 3. Total War on Taipei: China Explores Elevating Cognitive Effects into Its Vision of Warfare
**Source:** War on the Rocks  
**Link:** [Total War on Taipei: China Explores Elevating Cognitive Effects into Its Vision of Warfare](https://warontherocks.com/total-war-on-taipei-china-explores-elevating-cognitive-effects-into-its-vision-of-warfare/)  

**So What:** China's exploration of cognitive warfare strategies may indicate a significant shift in its military doctrine and pose new challenges for Taiwan and the US

China's concept of cognitive warfare aims to break the will to fight by targeting the operational system and cognitive cohesion of the enemy. This approach may be particularly effective in a conflict with Taiwan, where the goal is to rapidly capture and subdue the city. The development of cognitive warfare strategies may also have implications for the use of AI in military contexts and the role of information operations in modern warfare.
