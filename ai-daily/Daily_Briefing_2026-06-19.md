## 1. China Seeks A.I. Independence, Weakening Trump’s Leverage
**Source:** Center for Security and Emerging Technology  
**Link:** [China Seeks A.I. Independence, Weakening Trump’s Leverage](https://cset.georgetown.edu/article/china-seeks-a-i-independence-weakening-trumps-leverage/)  

**So What:** China's efforts to build a domestic A.I. ecosystem could reduce US leverage over Chinese tech companies

China's acceleration of domestic A.I. development, particularly through companies like DeepSeek and Huawei, indicates a strategic shift to mitigate the impact of US export controls. This move could not only reduce US influence over Chinese tech companies but also potentially create a competitive A.I. ecosystem that challenges US dominance. As a result, the US may need to reassess its export control strategy to effectively counter China's growing tech independence.

**Watch For:** Further investments by Chinese companies in domestic A.I. chip development and potential partnerships with other nations to circumvent US export controls
