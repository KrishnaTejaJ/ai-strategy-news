## 1. China Seeks A.I. Independence, Weakening Trump’s Leverage
**Source:** Center for Security and Emerging Technology  
**Link:** [China Seeks A.I. Independence, Weakening Trump’s Leverage](https://cset.georgetown.edu/article/china-seeks-a-i-independence-weakening-trumps-leverage/)  

**So What:** China's accelerated efforts to build a domestic AI ecosystem undermine US leverage in the tech competition

China's push for AI independence is a direct response to US export controls, and the development of alternatives to American chips by companies like DeepSeek and Huawei could reduce China's reliance on US technology. This shift may also create new opportunities for China to export its own AI technologies, potentially altering the global tech landscape. The success of China's domestic AI ecosystem could also have second-order effects on the global semiconductor supply chain

**Watch For:** Monitoring the progress of Chinese companies like DeepSeek and Huawei in developing alternative chips and AI technologies
