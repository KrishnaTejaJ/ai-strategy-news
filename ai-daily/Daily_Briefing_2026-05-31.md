## 1. China Seeks A.I. Independence, Weakening Trump’s Leverage
**Source:** Center for Security and Emerging Technology  
**Link:** [China Seeks A.I. Independence, Weakening Trump’s Leverage](https://cset.georgetown.edu/article/china-seeks-a-i-independence-weakening-trumps-leverage/)  

**So What:** China's pursuit of AI independence undermines US leverage over China's tech sector

China's efforts to build a domestic AI ecosystem, including the development of alternatives to American chips, reduces its reliance on US technology and limits the effectiveness of US export controls. This shift has significant implications for the US-China tech competition, as it threatens to erode US advantages in the sector. As China continues to invest in its domestic AI capabilities, the US may need to reassess its strategy for competing with China in the tech sector.

**Watch For:** Further investment by China in domestic AI chip development and potential responses by the US to counter China's growing tech independence
