## 1. China Seeks A.I. Independence, Weakening Trump’s Leverage
**Source:** Center for Security and Emerging Technology  
**Link:** [China Seeks A.I. Independence, Weakening Trump’s Leverage](https://cset.georgetown.edu/article/china-seeks-a-i-independence-weakening-trumps-leverage/)  

**So What:** China's accelerated efforts to build a domestic AI ecosystem undermine US leverage in the tech competition

China's development of alternatives to American chips, such as those by DeepSeek and Huawei, reduces its dependence on US technology and erodes the effectiveness of US export controls. This shift has significant implications for the US-China tech competition, as it enables China to pursue its AI ambitions with greater autonomy. The success of these efforts will depend on China's ability to overcome existing technological gaps and develop a robust domestic semiconductor industry

**Watch For:** Monitor China's progress in developing its domestic AI ecosystem, particularly in terms of advancements in chip manufacturing and AI software development

## 2. Beyond AI: What the Pentagon is missing with its trimmed ‘critical technologies’ list
**Source:** Center for Security and Emerging Technology  
**Link:** [Beyond AI: What the Pentagon is missing with its trimmed ‘critical technologies’ list](https://cset.georgetown.edu/article/beyond-ai-what-the-pentagon-is-missing-with-its-trimmed-critical-technologies-list/)  

**So What:** The Pentagon's prioritization of critical technologies may overlook key capability gaps and hinder long-term military innovation

The Pentagon's decision to narrow its list of critical technologies may lead to a lack of investment in emerging technologies that are crucial for maintaining a competitive edge. This could create vulnerabilities in the US military's capabilities and hinder its ability to address future threats. Effective prioritization and funding of emerging technologies are essential for strengthening long-term military innovation and addressing overlooked capability gaps

**Watch For:** Track changes in the Pentagon's funding and prioritization of emerging technologies, and assess the impact on the US military's capabilities and preparedness
