**Link:** [China Seeks A.I. Independence, Weakening Trump’s Leverage](https://cset.georgetown.edu/article/china-seeks-a-i-independence-weakening-trumps-leverage/)

## 3. A Taiwan Crisis and America’s Industrial Base
**Source:** The Diplomat  
**Link:** [A Taiwan Crisis and America’s Industrial Base](https://thediplomat.com/2026/06/a-taiwan-crisis-and-americas-industrial-base/)  

**So What:** A Taiwan crisis would expose the vulnerabilities of the US industrial base, particularly in semiconductor supply chains

A crisis in Taiwan would have significant implications for global semiconductor supply chains, highlighting the risks of offshoring and the need for the US to reassess its industrial base. This could lead to increased investment in domestic chip production and a reevaluation of US-China tech competition. The US government may need to develop strategies to mitigate these risks and ensure supply chain resilience.

**Watch For:** US government initiatives to strengthen domestic semiconductor production and reduce reliance on foreign suppliers
