## 1. China Seeks A.I. Independence, Weakening Trump’s Leverage
**Source:** Center for Security and Emerging Technology  
**Link:** [China Seeks A.I. Independence, Weakening Trump’s Leverage](https://cset.georgetown.edu/article/china-seeks-a-i-independence-weakening-trumps-leverage/)  

**So What:** China's pursuit of AI independence undermines US leverage in tech competition

China's efforts to build a domestic AI ecosystem, including developing alternatives to American chips, signify a strategic move to reduce dependence on US technology. This shift could have second-order effects on the global semiconductor supply chain, potentially altering the balance of power in US-China tech competition. As China progresses in its AI development, the US may face increasing challenges in exerting influence over China's tech sector.

**Watch For:** Monitor China's progress in developing domestic AI technologies and assess the impact on US export controls
