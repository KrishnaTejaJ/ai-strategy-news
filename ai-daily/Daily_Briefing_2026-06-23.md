**Link:** [China Seeks A.I. Independence, Weakening Trump’s Leverage](https://cset.georgetown.edu/article/china-seeks-a-i-independence-weakening-trumps-leverage/)  

**So What:** China's pursuit of AI independence undermines US leverage in the tech competition between the two nations

China's efforts to build a domestic AI ecosystem, driven by companies like DeepSeek and Huawei, could reduce its reliance on American chips and mitigate the impact of US export controls. **Watch For:** Monitoring the progress of Chinese companies in developing domestic AI capabilities and the potential response from the US government to maintain its technological advantage
