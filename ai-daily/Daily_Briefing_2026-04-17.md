**Link:** [AI & Tech Brief: Congress’s crackdown on global chip equipment](https://cset.georgetown.edu/article/ai-tech-brief-congresss-crackdown-on-global-chip-equipment/)  

**So What:** This matters as it may escalate US-China tech competition and impact semiconductor supply chains

## 3. The US military needs a commercial backup plan for an AI chip crisis
**Source:** Center for Security and Emerging Technology  
**Link:** [The US military needs a commercial backup plan for an AI chip crisis](https://cset.georgetown.edu/article/the-us-military-needs-a-commercial-backup-plan-for-an-ai-chip-crisis/)  

**So What:** This matters as it underscores the critical importance of semiconductor supply chain resilience for US military AI capabilities

The US military's reliance on advanced AI chips makes it vulnerable to supply chain disruptions, which could have significant implications for national security. Developing a commercial backup plan for AI chip production could help mitigate this risk and ensure the continued availability of critical AI technologies. **Watch For:** US military and industry efforts to develop backup plans for AI chip production and potential partnerships with commercial semiconductor manufacturers
