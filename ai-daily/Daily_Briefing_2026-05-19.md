**Source:** Center for Security and Emerging Technology  
**Link:** [China Seeks A.I. Independence, Weakening Trump’s Leverage](https://cset.georgetown.edu/article/china-seeks-a-i-independence-weakening-trumps-leverage/)  

**So What:** China's push for AI independence undermines US leverage in the tech competition

China's efforts to develop a domestic AI ecosystem, including alternatives to American chips, could reduce the effectiveness of US export controls and weaken the US's negotiating position in the tech competition.
