## 3. The US military needs a commercial backup plan for an AI chip crisis
**Source:** Center for Security and Emerging Technology  
**Link:** [The US military needs a commercial backup plan for an AI chip crisis](https://cset.georgetown.edu/article/the-us-military-needs-a-commercial-backup-plan-for-an-ai-chip-crisis/)  

**So What:** This matters because it highlights the critical challenge of ensuring AI model adaptability in crises, which could determine US military readiness and effectiveness.

The ability to rapidly retrain AI models is essential for maintaining battlefield adaptability, and the US military's reliance on specialized AI chips creates a vulnerability in the event of a supply chain disruption. Developing a commercial backup plan could mitigate this risk, but it will require significant investment in alternative chip technologies and retraining protocols. The success of this effort will depend on collaboration between the US military, chip manufacturers, and AI developers.

**Watch For:** US military initiatives to develop alternative chip technologies and retraining protocols for AI models.
